Introduction

HyperNova is an Indian Space Exploration corp., a provider of space transportation services, and is headquartered in Elysium, Mars. HyperNova was found in 2046 by Gourav Singh with the goal of introducing revolutionary technologies to transform the future of space travelling. HyperNova manufactures spaceships like Jupytor5 and Jupytor9, crew spacecrafts, like Hercules and Lyra, and spacebots like Scoutbot and Forton.

HyperNova's achievements include the first privately funded plasma-propellant spacecraft to reach the planet, Wolf 1061, carrying 6 crew members, the first private company to communicate with extraterrestial intelligent life form, the first space organization to experiment with the wormhole found near Alpha centuri, and the first space corp. to build Dyson sphere around the Earth.

About CEO

Gourav Singh

Gourav Singh Chauhan, born on 22 May 2003, is an enterpreneur, investor, and business magnate. He is the founder, CEO, and chief Engineer at HyperNova. Gourav was born and raised in Rajasthan, India. He did his schooling from Kendriya Vidyalaya. He completed his degree of Bachelor's in Technology from Lovely Professional University. During his post graduation in AI, he decided to pursue a business career.

In 2046, Gourav founded HyperNova, since then he has been contrubuting in the field of space exploration. He is also known to help poor kids achieve their goals by providing them with better education. Also, Gourav is an animal-lover, and henceforth he established an Oasis on Mars for animals.

Gourav is currently working on a project, on success of which, Humans will be able to travel through wormholes to reach their desired location in Universe.
